# Summit Karakoram


Summit Karakoram is an established, licensed (Pakistan Department of Tourist Services # 1152) outfitter/guide to major mountaineering expeditions in the Karakoram & Himalaya Range. Since its commence Summit Karakoram has provided support for successful attempts on K2, Nanga Parbat, Gasherbrum 1, Gasherbrum 2, Broad Peak, and numerous other peaks. In addition to mountaineering support, Summit Karakoram provides trekking or hiking support, enabling our clients to view some of the most spectacular scenery in the Karakoram Range, including Concordia (from which no fewer than four 8,000 meter-plus peaks are visible), K2 Base Camp/Milky Memorial, and Gondogoro Pass.
